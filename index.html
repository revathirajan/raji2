Our Hotel

Your luxury beach holiday destination in Goa

An exquisite stay experience awaits you at Park Hyatt Goa Resort and Spa, one of the best beach resorts in Goa. Strategically located close to the airport, a pristine beach and 45 acres of landscaped gardens form the beautiful backdrop at our South Goa beach resort. From spacious guestrooms with spa-inspired bathrooms to beautiful spaces for events and weddings, experience our impeccable attention to detail and personalised service when you stay with us.

Accessibility

At Park Hyatt Goa Resort and Spa, it is our constant endeavour to ensure that every guest experience is testimony to Hyatt hospitality.We offer two beautiful rooms specially designed for physically challenged guests. These rooms are located on the ground floor, close to the fire exits and near the main lobby and the Village Square.
